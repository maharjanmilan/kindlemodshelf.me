Tower of Hanoi for Kindle

Tower of Hanoi is the classic logic puzzle: move the stack of disks to another peg, one at a time, without placing a larger disk on a smaller one.

Features

  • Classic Tower of Hanoi gameplay
  • Multiple difficulty options (vary disk count)
  • Move counter to track efficiency
  • E-ink-friendly visuals

Requirements

  • Jailbroken Kindle with Mesquite support
  • Illusion framework compatibility

How to Install

  1. Download tower-of-hanoi 2.zip
  2. Unzip the file on your computer (uncompressed folder + .sh script)
  3. Place the .sh script and the folder in your Kindle’s documents folder
  4. Disconnect your Kindle
  5. Tap the new book in your Kindle library to launch

How to Play

  • Move all disks from the first peg to the last peg
  • Only one disk may be moved at a time
  • No larger disk may be placed on a smaller disk
  • Minimum moves for n disks is 2^n − 1
