Hangman for Kindle

Hangman is the classic word-guessing game. Pick letters and try to solve the hidden word before you run out of chances.

Download

  • Download hangman.zip

Features

  • Classic Hangman gameplay
  • Large word bank
  • On-screen A–Z keyboard (button layout)
  • Visual gallows builds with wrong guesses
  • Tracks guessed letters

Requirements

  • Jailbroken Kindle with Mesquite support
  • Illusion framework compatibility

How to Install

  1. Download hangman.zip
  2. Unzip the file on your computer (uncompressed folder + .sh script)
  3. Place the .sh script and the folder in your Kindle’s documents folder
  4. Disconnect your Kindle
  5. Tap the new book in your Kindle library to launch

How to Play

  • Tap letters on the on-screen keyboard to guess
  • Correct letters fill the word; wrong letters add to the gallows
  • Solve the word before you run out of guesses
