Snake for Kindle

Snake brings the classic arcade game to your Kindle e-reader. Eat food to grow, avoid walls and your own tail. Built with Illusion and tuned for e-ink.

Download

  • Download snake.zip

Features

  • Classic Snake gameplay
  • Arrow-button controls (▲ ◀ ▶ ▼)
  • Score tracking
  • Progressive difficulty as the snake grows
  • E-ink-optimized visuals

Requirements

  • Jailbroken Kindle with Mesquite support
  • Illusion framework compatibility

How to Install

  1. Download snake.zip
  2. Unzip the file on your computer (uncompressed folder + .sh script)
  3. Place the .sh script and the folder in your Kindle’s documents folder
  4. Disconnect your Kindle
  5. Tap the new book in your Kindle library to launch

How to Play

  • Use the on-screen arrow buttons to change direction
  • Eat the food to grow longer and increase your score
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail
Relive the classic Snake experience on Kindle!
