Creator & Maintainer

Kurizu

Kurizu is the creator of KAnki, bringing Anki's powerful spaced repetition system to Kindle devices in a way that feels natural and elegant on e-ink screens. His development work combines technical excellence with thoughtful user experience design.

Beyond creating the software, Kurizu provides consistent, patient support to users troubleshooting issues, implements feature requests, and continuously refines KAnki based on community feedback. His dedication to making learning accessible on Kindle devices has helped countless students and language learners.

The care and attention he brings to every bug report, feature discussion, and support thread exemplifies what makes open-source communities thrive.
