Home Screen Shortcuts

Home Screen Shortcuts are a quick and beautiful way to access your Kindle mods directly from the Home screen. Each shortcut launches an installed app or script instantly — no KUAL needed.

Installation Steps

  1. Select the shortcuts you want to add and download them from the list below.
  2. Copy them to the /mnt/us/documents folder on your Kindle.
  3. Disconnect your Kindle from your computer.
  4. After a few seconds, they will appear like books on your Kindle Home screen.

Shortcut Files

Alpine.sh Alpine launcher KOReader.sh KOReader launcher — opens KOReader directly from the Home screen. KindleFetch.sh KindleFetch utility launcher. Kterm.sh KTerm terminal launcher. shortcut_gambatte-k2.sh Game Boy emulator launcher for hardfloat Kindles. gargoyle.sh Text adventure (Infocom) emulator launcher. koreader_launcher_asap.sh KOReader ASAP launcher — opens KOReader instantly. screenControl.sh USB_Network_Toggle.sh image booklet KindleFetch2.sh other kterm scriplet. KOReader2.sh other koreader scriplet. Kterm2.sh other kterm scriplet. storagetool.sh

Notes

  • If a shortcut doesn't run, confirm the file is executable and located in /mnt/us/documents.
  • If a shortcut doesn’t appear, restart your Kindle and wait — it should show up within a minute.
  • These scripts are simple launchers that call the actual app start scripts on your device. You must have the corresponding app installed for them to work.
