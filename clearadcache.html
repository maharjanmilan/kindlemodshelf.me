Clear ads cache

This removes the cache ads on your kindle ui.

Download

remove_cache.zip

Features

Cleaned kindle ui

Requirements

Blocking Amazon

Installation

Unzip file

Move the script ClearCache.sh to the documents folder on you kindle

Move the script clear_cache.sh into the root of your kindle (otherwise known as /mnt/us/)

Click on the new book in your library called "Clear Cache & Thumbnails"

Notes

To undo just reenable Blocking Amazon
